Dipanjan Das ([@sherlock](https://x.com/sher10ckk)) is a Security Engineer at Microsoft, where he works on the Offensive Research and Security Engineering (MORSE) team to identify vulnerabilities in the Windows Core OS. With a PhD in System Security and a background in offensive security research, he previously spent time at Fortinet securing enterprise networking and cybersecurity products ranging from firewalls to endpoint detection systems. A graduate of UC Santa Barbara, Dipanjan is passionate about low-level systems, vulnerability hunting, and keeping the world's most widely used operating systems secure.
